Meet Our Founder

The Mind Behind SomaHire

A Legacy of Expertise in People Management

Donald R. Dixson

Founder, SomaHire LLC

Donald R. Dixson is the visionary founder and driving force behind SomaHire, a technology platform designed to revolutionize the way organizations approach people management and hiring. With a career spanning multiple decades in commercial art, corporate leadership, entrepreneurship, and management science, Donald has built a unique perspective on what it takes to build high-performing teams.

His journey began in the creative fields, where he developed an eye for detail and an understanding of human behavior. Over time, his career evolved into positions of increasing responsibility in corporate environments, where he observed firsthand the challenges organizations face when selecting and managing talent. These experiences ignited his passion for creating a better way.

Driven by the intersection of science and practical business wisdom, Donald founded SomaHire to address a fundamental question: How can organizations make better hiring decisions using measurable, science-backed methodologies? The answer became SomaHire—a platform that combines psychological insight, stress testing, and data-driven assessment to identify the right people for the right roles.

What sets Donald apart is his commitment to translating research into actionable tools. This dedication to evidence-based approaches is reflected in every aspect of SomaHire—from our stress testing methodology to our candidate assessment framework.

Today, as SomaHire serves organizations of all sizes, Donald remains actively involved in the company's direction and mission. His vision is clear: to help organizations build teams capable of navigating complexity, driving innovation, and achieving sustainable success. SomaHire is not just a business for Donald—it is the culmination of a lifetime of learning about what makes people and organizations thrive.
